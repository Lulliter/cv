designing and implementing diagnostic tools, advanced analytics and data
visualizations applied to economic development. Most of my research aims
at improving universal access to basic services (housing, water and
sanitation, energy, education and health).

I would like to further explore integration of survey data with other
sources --including social media, GIS and satellite imagery-- to uncover
various dimensions of poverty in vulnerable contexts.}
